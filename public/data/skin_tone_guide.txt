Warm Skin Tones:
  - Best colors: Earthy tones like olive green, brown, gold, rust, cream, and deep reds.
  - Avoid: Cool blues, silvers, and stark black.
  - Jewelry: Gold jewelry complements warm undertones.

Cool Skin Tones:
  - Best colors: Jewel tones such as emerald green, sapphire blue, royal purple, true red, and bright white.
  - Avoid: Orange, yellow, and most browns.
  - Jewelry: Silver and platinum jewelry enhance cool undertones.

Neutral Skin Tones:
  - Best colors: A wide range of colors work well, including soft versions of warm and cool colors. Muted greens, blues, and pinks are excellent.
  - Jewelry: Both gold and silver jewelry look good.
